About Move Forward Texas

Texas has long been a beacon of opportunity—a place where hard work, entrepreneurship, and individual initiative are rewarded. Move Forward Texas ensures our growth is sustainable and prosperity is accessible to all with practical, market-based solutions:

  • Transform housing policy to make homeownership and affordability achievable
  • Reduce regulatory barriers that stifle small business and entrepreneurship
  • Expand educational choice and opportunity for all Texas communities
  • Simplify taxation while maintaining vital public services
  • Promote sustainable growth through market mechanisms, not mandates

Housing Abundance

Texas's property tax crisis demands bold action. We support YIMBY reforms, zoning changes, and market-based approaches to dramatically increase housing supply and restore affordability.

Small Business & Entrepreneurship

Reducing occupational licensing barriers, simplifying regulations, and cutting through red tape to unleash Texas's economic energy—especially in underserved communities.

Education Innovation

Expanding charter schools, supporting educational choice, and ensuring every Texas child has access to quality education regardless of their zip code or family income.

Sustainable Prosperity

Market-based environmental solutions that reduce emissions while supporting economic growth—proving that prosperity and sustainability go hand in hand.
