About Move Forward Texas

Texas has long been a beacon of opportunity—a place where hard work, entrepreneurship, and individual initiative are rewarded. As millions continue moving to the Lone Star State, Move Forward Texas ensures our growth is sustainable and prosperity is accessible to all through:

  • Maintaining Texas's pro-growth, low-tax advantage while addressing property tax challenges
  • Ensuring housing abundance keeps pace with rapid population growth
  • Expanding educational opportunity and innovation across all communities
  • Protecting individual liberty while promoting community flourishing
  • Leading the nation in market-oriented, federalist policy innovation
