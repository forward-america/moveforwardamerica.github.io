About Move Forward Tennessee

Tennessee has long been a laboratory of innovation in American governance—from our constitutional commitment to low taxes to our pioneering education reforms. Move Forward Tennessee builds on this legacy, advocating for policies that:

  • Simplify and reduce tax burdens on working families and small businesses
  • Expand access to affordable housing and homeownership opportunities
  • Increase educational freedom and opportunity for all children
  • Ensure healthcare security without government overreach
  • Protect individual liberty while fostering community prosperity

Economic Growth & Tax Reform

Tennessee's no-income-tax advantage is a strong foundation. We'll work to build on this success with additional reforms that support small businesses, entrepreneurs, and working families.

Education & Opportunity

Expanding school choice, supporting career and technical education, and ensuring every Tennessee child has access to quality educational opportunities regardless of zip code.

Housing & Community Development

Addressing housing affordability challenges in growing cities like Nashville and Memphis while supporting rural community development across the state.

Healthcare Innovation

Promoting patient-centered healthcare solutions, price transparency, and market competition to improve quality and affordability for all Tennesseans.
