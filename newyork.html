About Move Forward New York

New York has always been a state of ambition, diversity, and opportunity. From Ellis Island to Wall Street to the innovation hubs of today, we've been a gateway for the American Dream. Move Forward New York works to restore that promise through practical reforms:

  • Address New York's housing crisis through supply-side reforms and reduced barriers
  • Simplify the tax code to reduce burden on middle-class families and small businesses
  • Expand educational opportunity and parental choice across all communities
  • Reduce cost of living through market competition and regulatory reform
  • Balance upstate and downstate needs through federalist principles

We're developing New York-specific policy priorities that address both urban and rural challenges. Check back soon for details on:

Housing Affordability

New York faces some of America's most severe housing challenges. We support zoning reform, streamlined permitting, and policies that encourage housing construction to bring down costs.

Tax Simplification & Relief

Reducing complexity in New York's tax system while providing meaningful relief to working families, small businesses, and entrepreneurs who drive our economy.

Educational Freedom

Expanding charter schools, supporting innovative educational models, and giving families real choices in their children's education—especially in underserved communities.

Upstate-Downstate Balance

Ensuring policies work for all New Yorkers—from the Bronx to Buffalo, Manhattan to the Finger Lakes—through federalist approaches that respect regional diversity.

Chapter Leadership Organizing
