About Move Forward California

California has always been America's innovation laboratory—from technology to entertainment to environmental leadership. Move Forward California channels that innovative spirit toward solving our state's biggest challenges with practical, market-based solutions:

  • Transform housing policy to make homeownership and affordability achievable
  • Reduce regulatory barriers that stifle small business and entrepreneurship
  • Expand educational choice and opportunity for all California families
  • Simplify taxation while maintaining vital public services
  • Promote sustainable growth through market mechanisms, not mandates

Housing Abundance

California's housing crisis demands bold action. We support YIMBY reforms, zoning changes, and market-based approaches to dramatically increase housing supply and restore affordability.

Small Business & Entrepreneurship

Reducing occupational licensing barriers, simplifying regulations, and cutting through red tape to unleash California's entrepreneurial energy—especially in underserved communities.

Education Innovation

Expanding charter schools, supporting educational choice, and ensuring every California student has access to quality education regardless of their zip code or family income.

Sustainable Prosperity

Market-based environmental solutions that reduce emissions while supporting economic growth—proving that prosperity and sustainability go hand in hand.
