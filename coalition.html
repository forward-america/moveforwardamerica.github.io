Americans for Tax Reform

Tax Reform & Economic Policy

Leading advocate for taxpayer protection and comprehensive tax reform aligned with our American Prosperity Act vision.

About the Organization

Americans for Tax Reform (ATR) is a coalition of taxpayers and organizations dedicated to a system of taxation that is simpler, fairer, flatter, more visible, and lower than it is today. Their advocacy for simplified tax structures and taxpayer protections aligns closely with our vision of a streamlined, growth-oriented tax system.

Key Alignment Points

  • Supports simplified flat tax structures similar to our 15% flat tax proposal
  • Advocates for reducing tax burden on small businesses and entrepreneurs
  • Promotes transparency and taxpayer accountability in government spending
  • Champions market-based economic solutions over government intervention
