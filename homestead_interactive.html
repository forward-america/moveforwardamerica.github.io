Part 2: Assistance

CRITICAL: ONLY in Homestead Communities

  • ✓ Up to $50K equity stake
  • ✓ Gov't silent partner (10-20%)
  • ✓ NO monthly payments
  • ✓ Pay back when you sell
  • ✓ You keep 80-90%

  • ✓ $15K one-time grant
  • ✓ Never repaid
  • ✓ Can combine with Tier 1
  • ✓ Income ≤ 120% median
  • ✓ Price ≤ $300K
